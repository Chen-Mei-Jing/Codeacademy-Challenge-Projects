MayMyo Fresh Coffee

Pyin Oo Lwin , formerly Maymyo, is a hill town of Myanmar , located in the Shan highland and at an altitude of above 3000 ft. There is a lot of variation in types and quality of coffee bean. Among them, Arabica beans are known as the highest quality coffee bean in the world, simply because of their better taste. Pyin Oo Lwin has well climatic condition for growing coffee, especially Arabica. MayMyo Fresh Coffee is one of qualified products in Myanmar.

To share our concept of freshness and differentiated value.

The name is why Maymyo Fresh?

The name “Maymyo” (former of "Pyin Oo Lwin") is more familiar, more expressive, more artistic and more attractive.
Our logo is basic themes of green and smart, clear and clever with single bean.

We offer quality coffees to our customer by the finest people.
Our employees, who we call partners, are at the heart of the Maymyo Fresh Coffee.
We believe in treating our partners with respect and dignity.
We value the local product of Myanmar.
